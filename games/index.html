Porsche Ring Apr 2018 Play Online

Windows Build
#Learning #Game #Prototype #Godot
My first 3D Godot game.
It's a racing game, for which I've created everything myself, except Godot-boy model and track texture. You can find more info on the car model in Blender section. The game features standard Godot car physics, it has a lap with checkpoints and lap timer. Actually fun to play. Try to get sub 20 sec. You can see an attempt of making a modular track on the side, but it was abandoned, as Godot didn't (doesn't still?) have hex-tileset workflow, and I didn't want to make is square-based.
It may be heavy on your PC to launch it in web, but you can try. Windows version is fine.

#Learning #Game #Prototype #Godot
My first "big" Godot game.
It is a 3x3 and 5x5 Tic Tac Toe game, where you can play hotseat with friend, 3x3 TTT against "simple AI" (it just fills random empty spots on it's turn), or launch 2 builds in local network and establish a chat between 2 computers. Unfortunately, you will have to manually make your opponent's moves on your field :)
It was supposed to have full multiplayer and a strong minimax AI, but I had no idea how to create them at the time. This is still certainly a non-featureless project in other spheres, and that's why I'm posting it.
You can try it online, but you should definitely check out the build and sources, and play with it's "multiplayer" stuff.
